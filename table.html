Skateboarding’s Holy Sites

Welcome to Sacred Grounds—your global guide to the most iconic terrain in street skating history. From marble plazas in Barcelona to stair-heavy chaos in São Paulo, this curated list maps out 20 legendary spots that shaped the culture, defined the style, and tested the limits of what’s possible on four wheels. It's kind of like urban architecture that turns everyday cityscapes into skateboarding folklore. Whether you're chasing lines, documenting skate footy, or just soaking in the hype, this is where the story begins. There's sunlit plazas and gritty underpasses, each spot on this list carries its own hype, its own history, and its own reason for being sacred. No parks. No pads. Just pure street.
