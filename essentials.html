Tools of the Trade

The Setup

Before the first push, before the first slam, there’s the setup the sacred assembly of deck, trucks, wheels, and wear that turns concrete into canvas. This page is a tribute to the gear that carries the grind: the splintered boards, the flat spotted wheels, the shoes with soles worn thin from flicks and fails. Each item here isn’t just equipment it’s a relic of resilience, a badge of style, and a tool of transformation. Whether it’s a cracked deck from a legendary session or the wax that made a ledge skateable, these essentials tell the story of street skating from the ground up.
